## Key dimensions


#### Outer Dimensions (The "Box")

* Length: 15.8mm
* Width: 31.8mm
* Height: 9.6mm (This is the height of the brick body, excluding the studs/pips).

------------------------------
#### Inner Cavity (The "Hole")

* Width: 13.4mm (Explicitly labeled in the center view).
* Length: 29.4mm (Calculated: 31.8mm total - 1.2mm wall thickness on each side).
* Height: 8.6mm (Calculated: $9.6mm total - 1.0mm top thickness).

------------------------------
#### Studs / Pips (Top Cylinders)

* Diameter: 4.8mm
* Height: 1.8mm
* Spacing: 8.0mm (Center-to-center).

------------------------------
#### Inner Tubes (Bottom Cylinders)

* Outer Diameter: 6.51mm
* Inner Diameter: 4.8mm (to fit the studs from another brick).
* Wall Thickness: 0.8mm (This is the dimension labeled at coordinate D-4).
