## Key dimensions


#### Outer Dimensions (The "Box")

* Length: 15.8mm
* Width: 31.8mm
* Height: 9.6mm (This is the height of the brick body, excluding the studs/pips).

------------------------------
#### Inner Cavity (The "Hole")

* Width: 13.4mm (Explicitly labeled in the center view).
* Length: 29.4mm (Calculated: 31.8mm total - 1.2mm wall thickness on each side).
* Height: 8.6mm (Calculated: $9.6mm total - 1.0mm top thickness).

------------------------------
#### Studs / Pips (Top Cylinders)

* Diameter: 4.8mm
* Height: 1.8mm
* Spacing: 8.0mm (Center-to-center).

------------------------------
#### Inner Tubes (Bottom Cylinders)

* Outer Diameter: 6.51mm
* Inner Diameter: 4.8mm (to fit the studs from another brick).
* Wall Thickness: 0.8mm (This is the dimension labeled at coordinate D-4).

## Steps

To create this LEGO brick in Tinkercad, follow this specific order to ensure the alignment and "hollowing out" process works correctly.
Note: Use the Snap Grid (bottom right) and set it to 0.1 mm for precision.
1. The Main Body (The Shell)
Drag a Box (Solid) onto the plane.
Change dimensions to Length: 31.8 mm, Width: 15.8 mm, and Height: 9.6 mm.
2. The Interior Cutout (The Hole)
Drag a Box (Hole) onto the plane.
Change dimensions to Length: 29.4 mm and Width: 13.4 mm.
Set the Height to 9.6 mm (it just needs to be tall enough to cut through).
Alignment: Select both boxes, click the Align tool, and center them on the Length and Width axes.
The Offset: Lift the Hole box down by 1.0 mm (using the black cone above the shape) so it doesn't cut through the top of the brick.
Select both and click Group.
3. The Top Studs (Pips)
Drag a Cylinder (Solid) onto the top of brick.
Change dimensions to Diameter: 4.8 mm and Height: 1.8 mm.
Positioning: Place the first stud at the corner (

 from the edges to the center, as per the diagram).
Duplicate: Use Ctrl + D and move the copy 8.0 mm to create the row. Repeat until you have all 8 studs.
Select the brick and all studs and Group.
4. The Inner Tubes (Underside)
Drag a Cylinder (Solid) onto the bottom of the brick. Dimensions: Diameter: 6.51 mm, Height: 8.6 mm.
Drag a second Cylinder (Hole). Dimensions: Diameter: 4.8 mm, Height: 9.0 mm.
Align these two cylinders to their centers and Group them to create a tube.
Positioning: Flip the view to the bottom. Place the tube centered between the studs (the diagram shows them spaced 8.0 mm apart).
Duplicate the tube twice to create the 3 tubes seen in the drawing.
Final Step: Select everything and Group.
