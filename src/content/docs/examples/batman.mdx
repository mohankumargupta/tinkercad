## Steps

#### Step 1

Get batman logo SVG from [here](https://worldvectorlogo.com/logo/batman-5/)

#### Step2 

Import SVG into  Tinkercad 

<Image src={base} alt="batman"/>

Do a uniform resize (press Alt+Shift+ click and drag corner white square icon on imported shape) 
to reduce the size.

<Image src={imported} alt="batman imported"/>

### Step 3 

You will notice that the outside ring/tube and the inner logo is one shape, and can't be ungrouped.
We will need to force the ungrouping. 

Drag a cylinder onto workplane. The job of the cylinder is to entirely cover the inner batman logo while
not touching the outer ring in order to separate the two.

<Image src={cylinder} alt="inner shape"/>

<Image src={cylinder2} alt="top view inner shape"/>
